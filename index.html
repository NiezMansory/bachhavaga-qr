Bacchavaga – The Heritage of Olive Oil

Organic extra virgin olive oil from Béja, Tunisia — hand-harvested, cold-extracted, and bottled at origin.

For generations, our family has cultivated olive trees in Béja, Tunisia, using traditional hand-harvesting. Bacchavaga celebrates this heritage by bringing you organic extra virgin olive oil, pressed from the finest olives. Every drop reflects history, tradition, and purity.

Cold-Extracted Single Origin First Harvest Unfiltered

Our Harvest

Hand harvesting protects the fruit and the trees. Olives are collected on clean nets and rushed to the mill for low acidity and fresh aromas.

Family Legacy

A family tradition—from grandfather to father to son—passing down trees and methods to ensure authentic flavor and respectful stewardship of our land.

Bottled at Origin

Bottled in Tunisia to lock in freshness. Each batch is traceable to our groves, with minimal handling from tree to bottle.

Quality & Recognition

Our meticulous approach results in a balanced, peppery finish and rich green-fruit notes. Bacchavaga is crafted to meet rigorous extra virgin standards and is continuously tested for quality.
