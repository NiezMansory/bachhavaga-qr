Extra-virgin olive oil from Béja, Tunisia

Single-origin · Cold-extracted · Family owned

Organic Olive Trees

Tunisia is home to some of the world’s oldest organic olive trees, thriving in mineral-rich soils and Mediterranean sun.

Traditional Harvest

Our olives are hand-picked using traditional methods to protect both fruit and trees, ensuring exceptional quality.

Cold Extraction

Within hours of harvest, we cold-extract to preserve aromas, polyphenols, and the vibrant green-gold color.

Taste & Pairing

Balanced fruitiness with gentle bitterness and a peppery finish; perfect for salads, grilled fish, and dipping.

Origin & Family

Grown and bottled by our family in Béja, north-west Tunisia, where the Mediterranean breeze shapes a unique terroir.
